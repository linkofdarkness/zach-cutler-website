Welcome! I'm Zach Cutler, and this is my new tech blog.

After 10+ years of building software professionally, I've decided it's time to start writing about the things I discover, the projects I work on, and the knowledge I think is worth sharing with other developers.

<!-- truncate -->

## What to Expect

This blog will cover a mix of topics, but you can generally expect posts in three categories:

### Discoveries

New tools, libraries, patterns, or techniques I've come across. The web ecosystem moves fast and there's always something interesting around the corner. When I find something that makes my life easier or changes how I think about a problem, I'll write about it here.

### Project Walkthroughs

I'll share retrospectives and deep-dives on things I've built. What worked, what didn't, and what I'd do differently. These will span the stack — from ASP.NET Core APIs to Angular and React frontends, from SQL Server queries to Blazor components.

### Knowledge Sharing & PSAs

Tips, gotchas, and heads-up posts for fellow developers. The kind of thing you'd share with your team on Slack, but longer form and searchable.

## The Stack

For the curious, this site is built with [Docusaurus](https://docusaurus.io/) — a static site generator that lets me write posts in Markdown and commit them straight to the repo. No database, no CMS, no friction. Just write, commit, and deploy.

Thanks for stopping by. I hope you find something useful here.
